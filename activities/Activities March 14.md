$$\dfrac{dx}{dt} = x - 2y$$

$$\dfrac{dy}{dt} = -6x - 3y$$

$$\dfrac{dx}{dt} = 3x + y$$

$$\mathbf{x}(t) = te^{3t} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + e^{3t} \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$$
